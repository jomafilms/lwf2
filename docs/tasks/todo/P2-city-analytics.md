## Problem

Bob (City Councilor) and the CWPP need data: how many properties are compliant, what's the coverage rate, are we progressing toward 90-by-10? City planners need analytics to justify funding and measure impact.

## Proposed Changes

### 1. City admin org
- Org type "city"
- City-wide view of all registered properties
- Map view with parcel-level compliance coloring

### 2. Analytics dashboard
- Total registered properties vs total parcels in city
- Compliance rates by neighborhood/zone
- Progress toward CWPP 90% goal (with projection)
- Most common plants being selected
- Nursery utilization (which nurseries getting the most business)

### 3. Grant documentation
- "X homeowners registered in the last quarter"
- "Y% compliance achieved in Zone A"
- "Z properties went from non-compliant to compliant"
- Export as PDF for grant applications

### 4. QR campaign management
- Generate QR codes linking to property registration
- Track scan → registration conversion
- Mailer campaign templates
